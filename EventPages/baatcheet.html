The Open Mic is an open stage for all talent like poetry, storytelling, stand-up comedy, Mimicry, Beat boxing; rapping or any other unique talent you have. You get the chance to perform in front of a live audience.

Rules

  • There will be only one round to perform
  • Time limit: 5 minutes
  • Any type of musical instrument is not allowed during the event
  • Your content should not contain vulgarity, profanity or any other negative material.
  • Selection of participants is solely based on their content.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. He/She should bring 2 passport size photos and an official identity proof.
