Description

This competition is a platform for artists to showcase their skills, creativity and imagination as they bring to life the tales etched on the skin, with every stroke of the brush. The skin serves as a canvas, where the paint and brush act as the medium to create stunning masterpieces that will leave a lasting impression. So come on, unleash your imagination and let's see how you can transform skin into a work of art that will stand the test of time.

Rules

  • This is a PAIR Team event. You can participate in teams of 2 only including the person on whom the tattoo will be made. Only one submission per team is allowed.

  • Vulgarity is strongly prohibited. Any obscenity will lead to debarring of the participants from the contest.

  • Any unfair practices would lead to immediate disqualification of the team. The decision of the judges will be final and binding. No protest against any judgement shall be entertained under any circumstances.

  • The participants should reach the venue 60 minutes before the event begins. The event timings would be informed well in advance.​


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. All participants must carry a valid identity card (Aadhar, PAN Card, Driving License etc.)​
