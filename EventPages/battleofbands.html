Description

Elements Culmyca is proud to present "Battle of the Bands," an event that provides a platform for musicians to showcase their talent and compete for the coveted title of "Best Band." The competition is exclusively open to individuals aged 18 years and above.


Prizes

Prizes are worth INR 5,000.

Rules

  • The competition will consist of one round where each participating band will perform a set of Western music.
  • Duration: 10 minutes (including set-up and clearance time).
  • A team can consist of 9 participants maximum (this includes singers and Accompanists) A maximum of 3 accompanists are allowed. Medley of songs is allowed. Karaoke or Programmed Music is strictly not allowed.


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. He/She should bring 2 passport size photos and an official identity proof.
